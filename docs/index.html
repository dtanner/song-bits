Song Bits — Privacy Policy

Song Bits does not collect any data. Period.

Your recordings, folders, and notes are stored on your device, or — if you choose a folder in iCloud Drive — in your own iCloud storage, where they sync between your devices through your Apple account. They are never sent to us or to anyone else. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and makes no network connections of its own.

The microphone is used only while you record, and the audio goes straight into a file that stays under your control. Deleting the app (and any files you placed in iCloud Drive) removes everything.

Questions? Email dan@dantanner.com.

Effective July 7, 2026.
